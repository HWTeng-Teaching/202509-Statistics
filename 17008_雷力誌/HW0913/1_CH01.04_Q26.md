### b. What proportion of the children were 35 months or older, but less than 45 months of age when first enrolled in preschool? 

| 年齡區間 | 人數 | 百分比 |
|----------|------|--------|
| < 35個月 | 12 | 24.0% |
| **35-44個月** | **27** | **54.0%** |
| ≥ 45個月 | 11 | 22.0% |
| **總計** | **50** | **100.0%** |

- 符合條件的兒童數量：27人
  
- $$P = \frac{\text{符合條件的兒童數量}}{\text{總兒童數量}}$$
$P = \frac{27}{50} = 0.54$
